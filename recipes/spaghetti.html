Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti good

Ingredients

  • Thin spaghetti
  • Spaghetti sauce

Directions

  1. boil water in medium sauce pan on high heat
    bring to medium-high when boiling
  2. break thin spaghetti in half and put into boiling
    water for 8-10 minutes
  3. put spaghetti sauce in small sauce pan on low heat
  4. serve food
