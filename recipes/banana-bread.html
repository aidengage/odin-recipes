Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread good

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs beat
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 3 mashed bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and grease 2 bread pans
  2. Mix everything in one bowl and pour into bread pans
  3. Bake in oven for 1 hour or until toothpick inserted
    into bread comes out clean
