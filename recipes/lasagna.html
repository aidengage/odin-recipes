Lasagna

Description

lasagna good

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 pound of shredded chedder cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. bring large pot to boil and cook pasta for 8-10 minutes
  2. preheat oven to 350 degrees F put a large skillit
    over a medium-high heat and brown beef, season
    with salt and pepper. stir in spaghetti sauce
    and garlic to let simmer for 5 minutes
  3. combine mozzarella, chedder, and ricotta in a medium
    mixing bowl and stir well
  4. in pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mix, and
    cheese mix until pan is filled
  5. bake in oven for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted
